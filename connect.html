Connect to TesNet Hotspot TesNet if you are connected to the network.

Buy data and log in using the hotspot portal below. Wi‑Fi login only works after you join our network.

Step 1 — Join the Wi‑Fi On your phone or laptop, open Wi‑Fi settings and connect to the TesNet@0207482341 hotspot network.
Step 2 — Open the login page The login page usually opens automatically. If it doesn’t, open your browser — you’ll see the same page as below.
Step 3 — Pay or enter your code Tap a package to pay with MoMo/card, copy your code, then enter it below to connect.
Important: If you are not on TesNet Wi‑Fi yet, you can still buy a package (packages open Paystack). To use your voucher and get internet, you must connect to the hotspot first — the “Connect to WiFi” button only works from the live hotspot portal.
