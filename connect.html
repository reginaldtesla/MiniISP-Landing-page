Hotspot setup

Connect to TesNet Wi‑Fi

Buy data and log in using the hotspot portal below. Wi‑Fi login only works after you join our network.

Step 1 Join the Wi‑Fi Open Wi‑Fi settings and connect to TesNet@0207482341.
Step 2 Open the login page The captive portal usually opens automatically. If not, open your browser — you'll see the page below.
Step 3 Pay or enter your code Tap a package to pay with MoMo or card, copy your voucher code, then enter it to connect.
Not on TesNet Wi‑Fi yet? You can still buy a package (Paystack opens in your browser). To use your voucher and get internet, you must connect to the hotspot first the "Connect to Wi‑Fi" button only works from the live portal.
